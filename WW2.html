WORLD WAR II

World War II was a global conflict that begun on the 1st of September 1939 and lasted to 2nd of September 1945. It was the deadliest conflict in human history. It involved the majority of world's nations including all the great powers, organized in two opposing military factions: The Allies and The Axis. Following the invasion of Poland, the war quickly spread to involve much of Europe, Africa, Asia and the Pacific. It has seen the most significant and devastating moments of our history, such as the Holocaust and the atomic bombings of Hiroshima and Nagasaki. Germany surrendered on the 8th of May 1945 , and at the end of August , Japan followed. This site was made with the purpose of always remembering the mistakes of the past, in naive hope that we will never repeat them.
